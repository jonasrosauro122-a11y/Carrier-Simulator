# CarrierOps Quoting Question Guide

This training version separates the quote workflow into **Auto** and **Home** after the user clicks **Start New Quote**. All customer fields are intentionally blank. VAs should practice asking the customer/agent for the information and entering only dummy training data.

## Auto Quote Sections

1. Account Setup: transaction type, effective date, producer/agency, quote source, named insured, DOB, contact info, mailing address, residence status.
2. Prior Insurance and Eligibility: prior carrier, prior policy number, expiration date, continuous coverage, lapse, cancellation/non-renewal, open claims, payment status.
3. Drivers and Household: primary driver, DOB, license state/status, years licensed, accidents, violations, major violations, SR-22, excluded drivers, undisclosed household members, driver notes.
4. Vehicle/VIN/Garaging: garaging address, vehicle year/make/model, VIN, ownership, use, commute miles, annual mileage, anti-theft, modifications, salvage title, loan/lease payoff.
5. Coverage and Discounts: BI, PD, UM/UIM, MedPay/PIP, comprehensive, collision, rental, roadside, paperless, autopay, bundle, telematics.
6. Underwriting Review: commercial/rideshare exposure, out-of-state garaging, vehicle not registered to insured, required documents, underwriter notes.

## Home Quote Sections

1. Account and Applicant: transaction type, effective date, producer/agency, quote source, named insured, contact info, mailing address, applicant type.
2. Property Location and Occupancy: property address, property same as mailing, form type, occupancy, ownership, closing date, short-term rental, home business, vacancy.
3. Home Characteristics and Protection: year built, square footage, stories, construction, foundation, roof year/type, protection class, fire distance, electrical/plumbing/HVAC updates, heating type.
4. Coverage and Mortgagee: Coverage A, other structures, personal property, loss of use, deductible, wind/hail deductible, water backup, liability, mortgagee name, loan number, mortgagee clause/address.
5. Prior Insurance, Claims and Discounts: prior carrier, prior policy number, expiration, continuous coverage, claims, lapse, cancellation/non-renewal, open claims, protective devices, paperless, autopay, bundle.
6. Hazards and Underwriting: coastal/brush exposure, pool, trampoline, animals, wood stove, liability hazards, unrepaired damage, flood zone, required docs, underwriter notes.
