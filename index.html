Training SOP

Carrier Portal Process Guide

New Quote Process

  1. Confirm named insured and effective date.
  2. Enter all required risk details before rating.
  3. Answer underwriting questions honestly and completely.
  4. Review referral/decline flags before presenting quote.
  5. Save quote or bind training policy only when data is complete.

How to Process Endorsement

  1. Search and verify the active policy.
  2. Confirm requestor authority and requested effective date.
  3. Collect exact change details and required documents.
  4. Review underwriting referral requirements and premium impact.
  5. Submit, document confirmation, and create follow-up if pending.

How to Cancel Policy

  1. Verify requestor identity and cancellation reason.
  2. Confirm replacement coverage when applicable.
  3. Secure signed cancellation request if required.
  4. Choose correct cancellation effective date.
  5. Document confirmation, refund details, and final status.

Payment Process

  1. Verify active/eligible policy status.
  2. Confirm payer authorization and payment amount.
  3. Post method, date, and confirmation number.
  4. Document remaining balance or next due date.

Auto ID Card Process

  1. Search active auto policy.
  2. Confirm vehicle VIN and effective dates.
  3. Generate printable ID card and document delivery.

Quoting & Remarketing Process

  1. Review renewal reason and updated risk details.
  2. Check carrier appetite and missing documents.
  3. Build quote strategy and save follow-up task.
