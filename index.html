How to Process an Endorsement

Carrier workflow
  1. Search and verify the policy number, named insured, policy status, and effective dates.
  2. Confirm the requested change and identify if it is flat, pro-rata, or subject to underwriting approval.
  3. Collect all required details and documents before submitting the endorsement.
  4. Quote additional or return premium when required.
  5. Document the change, submit for carrier processing, and notify the agency/client.

How to Cancel a Policy

Compliance workflow
Cancellation steps vary by state, carrier, and reason. This simulator is for training only. Always follow agency/carrier SOP.
  1. Verify policy, named insured authority, effective date, and cancellation reason.
  2. Check if the policy can be canceled flat, pro-rata, or short-rate.
  3. Collect signed cancellation request or proof of replacement coverage when required.
  4. Check open balance, pending payments, mortgagee/lienholder requirements, and refund handling.
  5. Submit carrier cancellation request, document the file, and set follow-up for confirmation.

Carrier Portal SOP Guide

Training workflow

1. Policy Search

Start every service request by searching the policy number or named insured. Verify LOB, status, effective dates, and authority before changing anything.

2. Auto ID Cards

Confirm active auto policy, VIN, effective/expiration dates, and vehicle details. Generate the card only after policy status and coverage are verified.

3. Payments

Identify payment type, amount due, due date, method, and reference number. Document authorization and save the receipt.

4. Endorsements

Clarify the requested change, collect required details, check underwriting impact, quote premium change, and document confirmation.

5. Cancellations

Verify authority, reason, requested date, signed request, replacement coverage, refund handling, and follow-up for carrier confirmation.

6. Remarketing

Compare current vs renewal premium, identify reason, gather updated exposures, approach markets, and summarize the best option.
